001 · Reaction Timer
AppADay · Day 1 · Category: Games & Interactive
How fast are your reflexes? Wait for the screen to flash green — then tap as fast as you can. Tracks your last 5 attempts with a live bar chart and a running average. Under 180ms is elite. No excuses, no retries. Just you and your nervous system.

▶ Play it live

How It Works
Tap anywhere to start. The screen will hold you in a random wait state between 1.5 and 4 seconds — long enough that you can't time it. When it flashes green, tap as fast as you can. Your reaction time is recorded in milliseconds.
Tap before the flash and it calls you out. No grace period.
Each result is added to a running bar chart of your last 5 attempts. A live average updates below the chart so you can track whether you're warming up or fading.

Scoring
Range Rating
Under 180ms🟢 Elite
180–239ms🟡 Sharp
240–319ms🔵 Average
320ms+🔴 Slow day

The average human visual reaction time is around 250ms. Under 200ms consistently puts you in the top tier.

Built With

HTML, CSS, JavaScript — no libraries, no dependencies. Uses performance.now() for millisecond-accurate timing.
